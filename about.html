About us...

NORTHSHORE ATHLETIC FIELDS was formed as a non-profit in 2005 to protect, improve and maintain the ballfields for the benefit of youth sports in the Northshore area and beyond. We operate under a long-term lease with King County Parks as a public-private partnership and raise all the funds to operate the park from individuals and organizations.
